The first aid kit

Even though the crash was probably rough. You didn't get seriously injured. just some cuts and bruises.

You see a first aid cabinet at the end of a corridor. The least you could do right now is clean yourself up a bit.

As you make your way to the cabinet the ship creeks at your every step. You gotta get out of this metal coffin soon.

You open the cabinet. But opening it set of some sort of chain reaction within the ship's already weak integrity.

Some panels fall, and the walls quickly come undone.

You try to move out of the way, but on of the big steele walls falls on you.

With all your strengh you try to hold it up long enough, but these walls weren't made to be lifted by a single man.

It slowly crushes you. Gasping for air only makes it hard to keep it up. You feel your bones beginning to crack.

You try to scream for help in the hopes that someone can hear you, but the crushing pain keeps you from forming any thought whatsoever.
